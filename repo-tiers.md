## Tier S++ — Career Defining

Incredible prestige, but expect months before meaningful contributions.

| Project | Why it matters | Difficulty | Resume ROI |
|---------|---------------|------------|------------|
| Kubernetes | Cloud infrastructure standard | ★★★★★ | 10/10 |

| Apache Kafka | Distributed systems | ★★★★★ | 10/10 |
| Redis | Infrastructure/database | ★★★★★ | 10/10 |
| Linux Kernel | Ultimate systems credibility | ★★★★★ | 10/10 |

**Reality:** These are resume candy but terrible ROI for getting started — competing with 1000s of drive-by PRs for tiny scraps.
